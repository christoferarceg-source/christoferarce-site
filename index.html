About

I go deep on hard problems — at the intersection of money, behavior, and technology.

From consulting and enterprise tech to fintech and banking, I've built a career at the intersection of financial products, customer behavior, and data-driven decision making.

Whether it's launching a credit card from scratch at a super-app, building the first debit-to-debit payment railway at a major bank, or working on cards and data at Capital One — I operate where strategy, technology, and the customer overlap, and translate that into real products.

I've shipped across four markets — Mexico, Brazil, Colombia, and the United States — navigating different regulatory environments, customer behaviors, and infrastructure realities in each one.

Outside work, I train for marathons — three and counting — and find the same clarity in long runs that I find in the mountains: wide open space, patience, and depth over noise.
